## Local Pipe Chain

![Annotated gate-valve to duct chain near SAMURAI](figures/samurai-gate-valve-pipe-chain.png)

The current working interpretation of the upstream chain is:

- Gate valve downstream boundary: `VF100`
- Pipe (1): `VG100-VF150` converter
- Pipe (2): `VG150-VF150` flexible pipe
- Pipe (3): `VG150-VF150` duct

For the dimensional standard and the quick VG/VF table, see [jis-flange-reference.md](jis-flange-reference.md).
