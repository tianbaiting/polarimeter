# 散射截面坐标系变化

计算相对论截面变化， 以及磁场偏转矩阵。



$$
\frac{\mathrm{d} \sigma}{\mathrm{d} \Omega}_{lab} =    \frac{\mathrm{d} \sigma}{\mathrm{d} \Omega}_{cm} \frac{\sin \theta_{cm} \mathrm{d} \theta_{cm}}{\sin \theta_{lab} \mathrm{d} \theta_{lab}}  
$$

## 实验室系质心系角度换算关系

$E_k=$380MeV的氘核经质子弹性散射，在实验室系中的四维矢量为：
$$
    p^{\mu}_{\text{总}lab}=\begin{pmatrix}
        m_D+E_k \\
        \sqrt{(m_D+E_k)^2 -m_D^2}\\
        0
    \end{pmatrix}
    +
    \begin{pmatrix}
        m_p\\
        0\\
        0
    \end{pmatrix}.
$$

在质心系中，总动量为零：
$$
    p^{\mu}_{\text{总}cm}=\begin{pmatrix}
        \gamma & - \gamma \beta & \\
        -\gamma \beta & \gamma & \\
        & & 1
    \end{pmatrix}    p^{\mu}_{\text{总}lab} = 
    \begin{pmatrix}
        E_{\text{总}cm} \\
        0 \\
        0
    \end{pmatrix}.
$$
易得质心系速度$\beta$,
$$
    \beta = \frac{\sqrt{(m_D + E_k)^2 - m_D^2}}{m_D +E_k+m_p}.
$$
入射氘核的质心系四动量为：
$$
    p^{\mu}_{\text{D}cm}=\begin{pmatrix}
        \gamma & - \gamma \beta & \\
        -\gamma \beta & \gamma & \\
        & & 1
    \end{pmatrix} p^{\mu} _{\text{D}lab}
$$
由于是弹性散射，在质心系中氘核的动量不变，所以说出射动量即是是入射动量，方向为$\theta_{cm}$,故氘核在质心系和实验室系的四动量分别为：
$$
    p^{\mu}_{\text{D}.cm.\text{f}} =\begin{pmatrix}
        E_{\text{D}.cm} \\
        P_{\text{D}.cm} \cos \theta_{cm} \\
        P_{\text{D}.cm} \sin \theta_{cm}
    \end{pmatrix}  \\
    p^{\mu}_{\text{D}.lab.\text{f}} = \begin{pmatrix}
        \gamma &  \gamma \beta & \\
        \gamma \beta & \gamma & \\
        & & 1
    \end{pmatrix} p^{\mu}_{\text{D}.cm.\text{f}} = 
    \begin{pmatrix}
        E_{\text{D}.lab} \\
        P_{\text{D}.lab} \cos \theta_{lab} \\
        P_{\text{D}.lab} \sin \theta_{lab}
    \end{pmatrix}
$$
解得：
$$
    \tan \theta_{lab} = \frac{P_{\text{D}.cm} \sin \theta_{cm}}{\gamma E_{\text{D}.cm} + \gamma \beta p_{\text{D}.cm} \cos \theta_{cm} }
$$

反解可得：
$$
    \theta_{cm} = \arcsin \left( \frac{\tan \theta_{lab} \gamma E_{\text{D}.cm}}{p_{\text{D}.cm}} \sqrt{1+\gamma^2 \tan ^2 \theta_{lab}^2}\right) + \arctan (\gamma \tan \theta_{lab})
$$


## 微分散射截面变化



微分散射截面的洛伦兹变化:
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\mathrm{d} \sigma}{\mathrm{d} \Omega}_{lab} &=    \frac{\mathrm{d} \sigma}{\mathrm{d} \Omega}_{cm} \frac{\sin \theta_{cm} \mathrm{d} \theta_{cm}}{\sin \theta_{lab} \mathrm{d} \theta_{lab}}  \\
&=\left(\frac{1}{\gamma^2 \tan ^2(\theta_{lab})+1} -\frac{\beta E_{\text{D}.cm}}{p_{\text{D}.cm} \left(\gamma^2 \tan ^2(\theta_{lab})+1\right)^{3/2} \sqrt{\frac{\gamma^2 \tan ^2(\theta_{lab}) (p_{\text{D}.cm}-\beta E_{\text{D}.cm}) (\beta E_{\text{D}.cm}+p_{\text{D}.cm})+p_{\text{D}.cm}^2}{p_{\text{D}.cm}^2 \left(\gamma^2 \tan ^2(\theta_{lab})+1\right)}}}\right) \\ \nonumber
 &\sin \left(\sin ^{-1}\left(\frac{\beta E_{\text{D}.cm} \gamma \tan (\theta_{lab})}{p_{\text{D}.cm} \sqrt{\gamma^2 \tan ^2(\theta_{lab})+1}}\right)-\tan ^{-1}(\gamma \tan (\theta_{lab}))\right)\gamma \csc (\theta_{lab}) \sec ^2(\theta_{lab}) \frac{\mathrm{d} \sigma}{\mathrm{d} \Omega} _{cm}\\
 &=\frac{0.92 \tan ^2(\theta_{lab})+0.58 \sqrt{1.18 \tan ^2(\theta_{lab})+1} \sqrt{\frac{1}{0.33 \tan ^2(\theta_{lab})+0.28}-2.5}-0.31}{1. \tan ^4(\theta_{lab})+0.51 \tan ^2(\theta_{lab})-0.28}\\ \nonumber
 & \sec ^2(\theta_{lab}) \sin \left(\sin ^{-1}\left(\frac{2.0 \tan (\theta_{lab})}{\sqrt{1.18 \tan ^2(\theta_{lab})+1}}\right)-\tan ^{-1}(1.1 \tan (\theta_{lab}))\right) \csc (\theta_{lab})\frac{\mathrm{d} \sigma}{\mathrm{d} \Omega} _{cm}
\end{align}
$$
